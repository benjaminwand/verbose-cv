Benjamin Wand - Verbose CV

As a generalist and prototyper, I publish very different things on various platforms and since the first time I was asked I'm struggeling with what should be on my cv. Obviously that depends on the occasion but it might be nice having a place where all the projects come together, for myself and for next time someone asks me "So, what do you do?"
This listing can be seen as an electronic Scanners Daybook, as Barbara Sher used to call it.
It will be a lot of work, for now this page is very incomplete. My goal is having a complete-ish 2020 soon and then add current things constantly and add older things when I feel like it. If you know me and there is a thing I should totally mention here - drop me a line!
