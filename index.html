Benjamin Wand - Verbose CV

As a generalist and prototyper, I'm publishing very different things on various platforms and since I ever was asked about it I'm struggeling with what should be on my cv. Obviously the later depends on the occasion but it might be nice having a place where it comes together, for myself and for next time someone asks me "So, what do you do?"
This list can be seen as an electronic Scanners Daybook, as Barbara Sher used to call it, and the dream would be that I'll never again have to assemble a cv because people will be satisfied with this list.
It will be substantial work to achive that, for now this page is very incomplete. My goal is having a complete-ish 2020 soon and then add current things constantly and add older things when I feel like it. If you know me and there is a thing I should totally mention here - drop me a line!
