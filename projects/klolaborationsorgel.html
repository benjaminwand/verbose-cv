Klolaborationsorgel

June - October 2021

The art project 'Klolaborationsorgel' by Hajo Toppius, Manuel Strube and many artists from Haus der Materialisierung used my 3d-printed organ pipes with tubes, pipes and toilet seats to build an installation (or musical instrument) that can be played by a group of people.
