Nachtigall

May 2018

Baroque organs often have stops for special effects, for instance mimicing bird noises, which are called 'Nachtigall' (nightingale). Information can be found on the German Wikipedia-page.

Seeing the video below inspired me to write my own file.

Here is the file on Thingiverse and there is an expolartion video:

The main reason why it looks like it looks is that this way it could be printed without support structures, everything is at 45° angeles.
My findings were that the object works but needs more air pressure than most of my files. It was used in the research phase of Klolaborationsorgel but not in the final installation.
