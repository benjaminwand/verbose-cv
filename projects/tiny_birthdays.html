Tiny Birthdays

Since Summer 2021

For several years I thought about how I can celebrate my birthday, given that I don't like parties. And then, during the pandemic, when we all thought about ourselves, I came to the conclusion that celebrating with everyone at once is just a tradition and I do want to see my friends but one at a time.

And so I invited friends to do a special thing together that otherwise would have deemed too much effort, for instance going to a more-fancy-than-usual restaurant together. Food and museums were often involved, I guess the thing farthest away from my usual activities was collecting trash in the river Spree . But it was fun!
