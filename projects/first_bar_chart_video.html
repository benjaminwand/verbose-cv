The first Bar Chart Video

May 12th, 2020

In 2019 I started watching a data visualisation course and other things that included pandas and matplotlib and never did anything with that knowledge. But now we have the corona-crisis and since I stare at data multiple times a day anyway, I thought I might as well do something with it. Turns out, with flourish the chart-making is very easy once one has cleaned the data. I used the dataset from Johns Hopkins, and will update it every now and then.
