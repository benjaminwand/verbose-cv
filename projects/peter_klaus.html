Concrete in burned wooden form work

November - December 2023

I made a concrete item in a wooden form work which was burned in the oven after six weeks of drying for architecture school, inspired by Peter Klaus Feldkapelle, which is highly praised in the German academic architecture scene.
Due to the incredible surface I consider it the most valuable item from the first semesters design class.
