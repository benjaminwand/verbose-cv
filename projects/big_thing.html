Dovetail printable large craft(s)

Summer 2023

I wrote a first prototype for the project of Kerry Williams from Alaska who would like to build large objects, like kayaks, from 3d printed puzzle pieces, so, now that more and more people have access to 3d printers, more people have the chance to build large things on their own. The first prototype is a sphere shaped puzzle that I wrote in OpenSCAD and the pieces are hexagons and pentagons, like a soccer ball / truncated icosahedron .

There will be more text later, for now this article is just a placeholder for the links to the project on GitHub and on Printables.
